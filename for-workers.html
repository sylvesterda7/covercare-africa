FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What documents do I need to join? ▾
You'll need a valid professional license or registration issued by your country's regulatory body, a government-issued ID, and proof of qualification. The verification process takes less than 24 hours.
How and when do I get paid? ▾
You can choose instant payout to your Mobile Money wallet for a small fee, or free 2–3 business day bank transfer. Payments are released once the facility confirms your shift is complete.
Can I cancel a shift I've booked? ▾
Yes, you can cancel up to 4 hours before the shift starts without penalty. Late cancellations may affect your reliability score.
Is there a minimum commitment? ▾
No. You work as much or as little as you want. There are no minimum hours or shift requirements.
How does the QR check-in work? ▾
When you arrive at a facility, show your unique QR code from the app. The facility scans it to confirm your arrival and start the shift.
