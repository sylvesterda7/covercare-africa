Frequently asked questions
How does CoverCare verify professionals? ▾
Every professional is verified against the Pharmacy Council Ghana database. We also verify government-issued IDs and professional licenses before they can accept shifts.
How much does CoverCare cost for facilities? ▾
Posting shifts is free. You only pay a platform fee when a shift is successfully filled — starting at 25% of the worker's pay. See our pricing page for details.
How quickly can I get a shift covered? ▾
Our average time to fill is under 2 hours for urgent shifts. Many shifts get matched within minutes of posting.
Is CoverCare available outside Accra? ▾
Yes! We're live in Accra, Kumasi, Tamale, Cape Coast, and Takoradi, with more cities coming soon.
