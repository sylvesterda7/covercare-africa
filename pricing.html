FAQ

Pricing FAQ

Is there a fee to post a shift? ▾
No. Posting shifts is completely free. You only pay the platform fee when a shift is successfully filled and completed by a worker.
How is the platform fee calculated? ▾
The platform fee is 25% of the worker's total pay on the Per Shift plan, 15% on Monthly, and custom on Enterprise. This fee covers matching, verification, payment processing, and support.
Can I switch plans at any time? ▾
Yes. You can start with Per Shift and upgrade to Monthly or Enterprise at any time. There are no long-term contracts.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, and card payments. Enterprise customers can request invoice-based payment terms.
Is there a refund if a worker doesn't show up? ▾
Yes. If a confirmed worker fails to show up, we waive the platform fee for that shift and prioritise finding a replacement at no extra cost.
