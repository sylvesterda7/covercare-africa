FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is it really free to post shifts? ▾
Yes. Creating an account and posting shifts is completely free. You are only charged a platform fee when a shift is successfully filled and completed by a worker.
When does the 25% fee apply? ▾
The 25% platform fee is calculated on the worker's gross pay and deducted when the worker completes their shift and payment is released. If a shift isn't filled, you pay nothing.
Can I switch plans later? ▾
Yes. You can start on the per-shift plan and switch to a custom monthly plan at any time. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.
Are there any hidden fees? ▾
No. There are no setup fees, monthly minimums, or cancellation charges. The only fee is the platform fee on completed shifts.
